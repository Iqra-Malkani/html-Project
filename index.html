WHO ARE WE :

Being the first to serve authentic South Indian cuisine in the heart of Downtown Montreal, Chennai Express strives to deliver authentic South Indian Cuisine with a plethora of flavors. With a menu consisting of various curries, dosas, idly, bajji, vada, kothu parottas, and much more, there will definitely be something for you to enjoy. We make sure to accommodate your dietary views (vegan, vegetarian, non-vegetarians, halal certified).
